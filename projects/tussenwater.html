2018
Video Installation

Tussenwater is a video installation made in collaboration with Merel Dames. The film shows close-ups of water reflections of various locations around Utrecht, The Netherlands. The water distorts the reflected environment, creating abstract, moving images. The sound is made from field-recordings from the same locations. They are processed, giving water sounds musical qualities and musical sound water-like qualities.

Tussenwater was shown in fortress Honswijk, May 2018. We made use of the architecture of the fortress to project image and sound, such as the arched seiling and a 30-meter long tunnel as shown below.
